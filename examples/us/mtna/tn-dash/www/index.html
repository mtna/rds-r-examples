Tennessee Dashboard

COVID-19, Google Mobility Report

This Rich Data Services powered dashboard combines data from the Tennessee State Health Department's COVID-19 County Level Case Information and the County Level Google Mobility Reports .

It has been designed with the open source rds-r library developed by MTNA to demonstrate RDS benefits and flexibility in terms of combining data from multiple sources for visualization or story telling puposes and rapid design.
